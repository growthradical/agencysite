# PAS — Issue 01 (July 2026) · Page-by-Page Plan

**Issue theme:** *After Copenhagen* — North Macedonia's World Cup dream ends in
the March play-off, the World Cup itself plays out without us (11 June–19 July),
and the question for July is the only one that matters: **what now?**

**Format:** 84 pages, perfect-bound print + page-faithful digital edition (PDF +
flipbook + companion web).
**Trim:** 230 × 300 mm (print). **Digital:** same proportions, exported at
2× for retina.
**Language:** Macedonian primary, English secondary (sidebars + full English
PDF edition).
**Cover star:** Eljif Elmas — the talisman who now carries the rebuild.

A reusable **house-style block** for all image prompts lives in
[`04-gpt-image-prompt-library.md`](04-gpt-image-prompt-library.md). Where a
prompt below says *"[HOUSE STYLE] +"*, paste that block first. Every prompt is
written to produce **text-free artwork with clean negative space**; all
lettering is added in layout.

**Legend for each entry:** Page(s) · Section · Working title · Brief · Word
count · Layout · Metadata · Image prompt.
