# PAS — Issue 01 (July 2026) · Page-by-Page Plan

**Issue theme:** *Повторно шампиони / Champions Again* — **Vardar reclaim the
Macedonian crown after a nine-year wait** and head into the **Champions League**,
while interest in football across the country booms. We celebrate the return of
a fallen giant, cover the local game match by match, mourn a legend (Vasil
Ringov, 1955–2025), and still face the national team's hard summer honestly.

**Format:** 84 pages, perfect-bound print + page-faithful digital edition (PDF +
flipbook + companion web).
**Trim:** 230 × 300 mm (print). **Digital:** same proportions, exported at 2×.
**Language:** Macedonian primary, English secondary (sidebars + full English PDF).
**Cover:** Vardar — *Champions Again.* The trophy lift / the captain in Vardar red.

A reusable **house-style block** for all image prompts lives in
[`04-gpt-image-prompt-library.md`](04-gpt-image-prompt-library.md). Where a prompt
says *"[HOUSE STYLE] +"*, paste that block first. Every prompt produces
**text-free artwork with clean negative space**; all lettering is added in layout.

**Legend per entry:** Page(s) · Section · Working title · Brief · Word count ·
Layout · Metadata · Image prompt.

> ⚠️ **Verify in press week:** Vardar's exact title number, the collapse-era
> timeline, the Champions League Q1 opponent/venue (draw was 16 June 2026; legs
> 7/8 & 14/15 July), squad and new signings, and all national-team facts. See the
> sources list in [`02-topic-list-july-2026.md`](02-topic-list-july-2026.md).
