## 2. Print specifications
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Trim size | 230 × 300 mm |
| Bleed | 3 mm all sides (236 × 306 mm) |

| Cover stock | 300gsm silk, soft-touch matte laminate + spot UV on masthead |

## 4. File-naming convention
```
PAS_I01_<page>_<slug>            e.g. PAS_I01_p17_coverstory_open
PAS_I01_<C1|IFC|IBC|C4>_<slug>   e.g. PAS_I01_C1_cover_elmas
IG_d<NN>_<slug>                   e.g. IG_d09_shkendija_leg1
```
- All editorial assets live under `issue-01/`. Social assets under
  `issue-01/social/` (create when producing).
- Keep a `_SRC` (layered/editable) and `_EXPORT` (flattened) version of each.
