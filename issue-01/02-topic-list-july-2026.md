## The defining context of the month
- **Vardar are champions of North Macedonia again** — their **first 1.MFL title
  since 2016–17, a nine-year wait** — after the club's financial collapse,
  relegation and near-extinction last decade. A fallen giant resurrected.
- **Vardar enter the UEFA Champions League** — first qualifying round, legs
  **7/8 July** (1st) and **14/15 July** (2nd). The draw was held 16 June 2026;
  winners advance to the Champions Path second qualifying round.
- **Interest in football is booming** across the country — attendances, youth
  participation, streaming and social communities all rising.
- **A legend was lost** — **Vasil Ringov (1955–2025)**, Vardar's all-time record
  scorer and Pančev's strike partner, died 23 December 2025.
- **The national team is in a slump** — out of the World Cup (4-0 to Denmark, 26
  March play-off), winless into the June friendlies (4-0 to Türkiye, 1 June).
- **The World Cup plays out without us** — finals 11 June–19 July; **final 19 July.**
