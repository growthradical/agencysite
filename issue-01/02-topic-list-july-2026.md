## The defining context of the month
- **North Macedonia is NOT at the 2026 World Cup.** Beaten **4-0 by Denmark** in
  the UEFA play-off semi-final at Parken (26 March 2026), after finishing third
  in qualifying Group J behind Belgium and Wales and reaching the play-offs via
  the Nations League path.
- **The team is in a slump** — a winless run extending through the June 2026
  friendlies, including a **4-0 loss to Türkiye (1 June 2026)** and a fixture vs
  Bosnia & Herzegovina (29 May 2026).
- **The World Cup plays out without us** — the finals run **11 June–19 July
  2026**; the **final is 19 July**. The diaspora watches the tournament we missed.
- **Club football provides the hope** — **Shkëndija**, 2025–26 **1.MFL champions**
  (their sixth title), enter Europe.
